Access to Blackboard

There are multiple ways to access blackboard. The easiest way is to go to myUoN and login using your student number and password.

Access to Blackboard

MyUoN is your dashboard which you can navigate to find numerous resources that you will need throughout your University degree. The red circle in the image shows where the blackboard link is located. Clicking this card will take you to blackboard.

Welcome Page

Welcome to Blackboard! This welcome page gives you a preview of student and study essentials. It also provides IT support where you can learn about the University Wi-Fi and how students can use Microsoft Office during their study. Clicking the link in the red circle will take you to your course sites.

Blackboard Home

This webpage contains links to all your courses and course information for this semester and previous semesters. The most important sections of this page are:

  • My Course Sites
  • My Other Sites

My Course Sites

This section lists the courses you are taking this semester. Clicking one of these courses will take you to the main hub for your courses. To see how to naviagate through your course page, keep scrolling below.

My Other Sites

Some of these sites listed are very important as you will be required to compelete some of these tasks before your first week of University. Below is a small description of some of these hubs which will be very important for students:

– Academic Learning Support
This will take you to the Academic Learning Support hub where you can receive help with the fundamentals of your courses and learn about the standard the University holds in regard to assignments and different assignment types.

– Consent Matters
The consent matters module needs to be completed by all students who are undertaking University at Newcastle Uni to ensure the understanding and importance of consent.

– MATH1110 Maths Placement Test
Some programs will require students to do MATH1110 (mostly Engineering and Science degrees) which will require some students to complete a placement test in order to be allowed to take this course. This test can only be taken once so make sure you practice before taking the exam.

– University Academic Inegrity Module
Before completing your first semester, the academic integrity module MUST be completed in order to receive your Semester 1 results. This is to ensure you have understanding of plagiarism.

My Courses

The pages for your courses will be your go-to domain for all your course learning and information. You can view the announcements from your course demonstrators, view lecture slides, access assignment information, check your grades, and review any previous recorded lectures. Click on any of the menu items on the left hand side to view their corresponding page.
