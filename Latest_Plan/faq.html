How do i get Overseas Student Health Cover?

Overseas Student Health Cover (OSHC) is insurance that helps cover the cost of medical care for international students while they are living in Australia, which in some instances, applies to pharmaceuticals and ambulance services. It is a mandatory requirement for all student visa holders in Australia. International students must maintain OSHC for the duration of their student visa, as do any family members, such as partners and children.

The University of Newcastle, is working with  Allianz Global Assistance to assist students to streamline this process. International students can signup with the help of the three steps provided below:

Step 1: Register for online services - Visit allianzassistancehealth.com.au and create an online account
Step 2: Download the My OSHC Assistant app
Step 3: Download the Doctors on Demand app

If you need help with any of these processes and want to know more about the benefits you can get from this insurance. you can visit the locations mentioned below.

  • Callaghan Campus, Hunter Building, UON Global (Monday to Wednesday 9am to 5pm)
  • Newcastle City Precinct NUspace on Thursday 10am to 4pm.

Where can i obtain my Student Id card?

For new students who just enrolled in university, getting a Student Id card is essential.
By having a Student Id card you can have access to university facilities.

Please read the infromation below:

  • In order to get your Student Id card you can visit this website and upload your photo here.
  • After signing up for a Student Id card you can grab your card between 9am and 5pm Monday to Friday at Student Central
  • If you have lost your Student Id card you can apply for a replacement card online
