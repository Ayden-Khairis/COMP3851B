General Questions

How do I get a Student ID card?

In order to get your Student ID card you can visit this website and upload your photo here. After signing up for a Student ID card you can grab your card between 9am and 5pm Monday to Friday at Student Central.

What is Blackboard?

Blackboard is an online tool which contains all your course information, course outlines, assessment requirements, lecture recordings, assessment submissions, and grades. To learn how to access and use this tool, click here to be taken to the Blackboard tutorial.

What is MyHub?

MyHub is another online tool where you can enrol in your courses, update personal details, pay fees and view your financial records, and view any scholarship information you may have. To learn how to access and use this tool, click here to be taken to the MyHub tutorial.


I'm nervous and feel like I might struggle during my first semester, who can help me?

The Academic Learning Support team can help you with English skills, Maths and Stats skills, Study skills, and can teach you learn how to research, critically think and analyse, and problem solve all for free! They provide workshops via Zoom, learning resources, guides and templates, email consultantions, and individual appointments. Feel free to contact this team if you ever need help with these aspects of your learning.

How do I view my timetable?

After selecting your courses for the semester, login to MyUoN and click the "myTimetable" card in the quick links section and you will be taken to your class timetable.

My timetable says I'm in Room CT201, what does that mean?

The first one or two letters at the start of the room name identifies the building. In this case, you would be in the CT building. The first numeral after the letters represents the floor number. In this case, you'd be on the second floor. Sometimes the floor number will be G or LG, which represents the "ground" and "lower ground" floors. The last two digits of the room name represents the room number. In this case you'd be in room 1 of the second floor which is room 201.
