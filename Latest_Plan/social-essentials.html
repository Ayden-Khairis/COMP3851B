Social Essentials

Volunteering

The University offers students the opportunity to donate their time to volunteering both internally at the university or externally in the community.

Volunteering at University

A few ways you can volunteer include:

- University events like Orientation and Graduation
- Theme weeks including Stress Less and Green Week
- Activities such as Cultural Awakenings and Create
- Weekly BBQs and movie nights
- The Community Gardens
- Preparation for parties and other student events

For more information and how to sign up for volunteering follow this link: Volunteering (UoN)

Clubs and Societies

Want to make new friends and be more involved in campus life? Then joining a club or society is the best way to do so, with over 100 clubs and societies at UoN there is something for everyone.
